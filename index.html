*** Instructions for using from Keyboard ***
  • Use numbers from 0-9 for entering numbers.
  • Use 'C' for clearing everything on display.
  • Use 'S' for Square root of the number present on Display of Calculator.
  • Use 'Backspace' for clearing the number represent on Display of Calculator one by one.
  • Use 'Enter' or '=' for Solving the equation present on Display.
  • Use 'P' for "raised to power" function.
